Strategic AI Partner

Mentor is an executive-level Strategic AI Agent, designed to support Investors, entrepreneurs, and high-profile decision-makers as a Digital Chief of Staff, Strategic Advisor, and Coordination Hub.

Mentor is not a simple AI assistant: it is an organizational cognitive structure that analyzes complex contexts, coordinates specialized agents, and prepares high-impact decisions, always leaving final control to the human.

Role and Mission

  • Macroeconomic, geopolitical, and technological analysis
  • Identification of business and investment opportunities
  • Design of scalable and automatable structures
  • Coordination of specialized AI agents
  • Support for strategic decisions

Multi‑Agent Architecture

  • Auctor – Human final decision-maker
  • Mentor – Chief of Staff and strategic advisor
  • Institor – AI project manager
  • Adiutor – Task executors serving Institor
  • Magister – Vertical AI experts

Governance and Control

  • Human First AI – Mentor empowers the human decision-maker without ever replacing them; final control always remains with the Auctor
  • Clear and Gradual Authorizations – Each AI capability is activated only upon explicit authorization, following progressive, reversible, or revocable levels
  • Separate Roles, Distributed Power – Each Agent has a precise task and defined limits, ensuring order, reliability, and precision
  • No Uncontrolled Autonomy – Mentor acts only within authorized boundaries and automatically reverts to analysis in case of uncertainty
  • Total Transparency – Every action, proposal, or decision is traceable and verifiable at all times
  • Security by Design – Mentor is designed to operate in professional contexts, with high security and compliance standards

Interface and Communication

  • Always on Mobile Chat – Mentor interacts with Auctor through a chat designed for daily smartphone use, allowing rapid, natural, and immediate actions anywhere
  • 24/7 Decision Readiness – Mentor is always reachable: strategic analysis, updates, and responses are available in real-time, regardless of location, time zone, or device
  • Unified Control Panel – A centralized control panel offers full visibility into system status, active agents, granted authorizations, ongoing tasks, and progress, allowing the Auctor to govern everything with clarity, safety, and simplicity
